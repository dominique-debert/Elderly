Community Champion

This badge is awarded to users who actively participate in community events and contribute positively to the Neighborly community. It recognizes your commitment to fostering a supportive and engaging environment for all members.

How to Earn

To earn the Community Champion badge, participate in at least 5 community events, post 3 helpful comments or discussions, and receive positive feedback from other users. Your contributions should reflect a genuine interest in community well-being and engagement.

Tips for Earning

Engage actively in discussions, share your experiences, and support fellow members. Attend a variety of events to broaden your community involvement. Offer assistance or advice when appropriate, and always maintain a respectful and positive attitude.
